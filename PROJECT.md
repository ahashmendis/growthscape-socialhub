# Growthscape Social Hub

## Vision

Growthscape Social Hub is an AI-powered Social Media Operating System.

It combines analytics, scheduling, AI, automation, reporting, planning, and content generation into one unified platform.

The goal is to become significantly more intelligent than Metricool while remaining intuitive and easy to use.
